SleepTax Explorer

This interactive tool lets you create your own speculative sleep-tracking device. It is based on SleepTax, a taxonomy developed from 350 real-world sleep technologies.

Instead of designing from scratch, you will move step by step through:

  • What the device looks like
  • What it tracks
  • Why people use it
  • Where it is used
  • How it operates over time

How it works

  • Follow guided steps
  • Each choice builds your device
  • Generate a speculative scenario at the end
